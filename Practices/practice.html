ROUTINE

Here's a simple and easy-to-understand routine:

1. Morning:

-Wake Up Early: Start your day with enough sleep. Aim for 7-8 hours.
-Hydrate: Drink a glass of water to rehydrate your body.
- Exercise: Engage in 20-30 minutes of physical activity, like a brisk walk or home workout.
-Breakfast: Have a balanced meal with protein, fiber, and healthy fats.

2. Daytime:

- Work/Study: Focus on your tasks, take short breaks every hour to stay productive.
- Lunch: Eat a nutritious lunch with a mix of veggies, lean protein, and whole grains.
- Stay Hydrated: Drink water throughout the day to stay hydrated.
- Snack Smart: If hungry, choose healthy snacks like fruits, nuts, or yogurt.

3. Afternoon:

- Short Walk: Take a brief walk to refresh your mind.
- Learn: Spend some time gaining knowledge, whether through books or online resources.

4. Evening:

- Dinner: Have a lighter dinner with lean protein and vegetables.
- Relaxation: Wind down with activities like reading or listening to music.
- Limit Screen Time: Reduce exposure to screens an hour before bedtime.

5. Night:

- Prepare for Bed: Establish a calming bedtime routine.
- Sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep.

Remember, consistency is key. Adjust the routine to fit your lifestyle, and listen to your body's needs. It's essential to balance work, relaxation, and self-improvement.
